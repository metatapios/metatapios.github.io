Matteo Pais (IPA: /matˈtɛo ˈpai̯s/, listen )

I am an Italian postdoctoral fellow at the National Institute for Astrophysics (INAF), at the Astronomical Observatory of Padua (OA-PD) in Italy, Veneto. Before returing in Italy, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem (HUJI) (2020-2024) in the group of Prof. Tsvi Piran, and a graduate student at Heidelberg University in the International Max Planck School for Astronomy and Cosmic Physics (2016-2020), Heidelberg Institute for Theoretical Studies (HITS) (2016-2017) and the Leibniz Institute for Astrophysics Potsdam (AIP) (2017-2020), in Germany.

My research focuses on high-energy astrophyiscs and general relativity. I'm a computational/theoretical astrophysicist working with codes and combining simulations, theory and analytical approximations. I'm mostly interested in gamma-ray bursts jets, compact objects and binary neutron star mergers[read more]

  • Statistics and machine learning
